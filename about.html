My Story

Hi, I'm Westley Kleinman, the founder, designer, and maker behind Elevon. I'm from Arkansas, where I grew up riding and racing in the NICA Arkansas League and discovered my love for mountain biking and trails.

These days, I'm a sophomore mechanical engineering student at Duke University, the president of Duke Club Cycling, a MaccabiUSA Pan American Games alumni, and a lifelong trail rider.

I started Elevon because I wanted to bring the trails and courses I love to life in a way you can hold, display, and celebrate. My very first customer was the NICA Arkansas League—the same league where I began racing. Since then, I've helped race directors, shops, and riders across the country bring their favorite trails to life, one map at a time.
