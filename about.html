About Elevon

Turning Topographical Trails into 3D-Printed Masterpieces

Hello! I’m Westley Kleinman—a freshman studying Mechanical Engineering at Duke University and proud native of Hot Springs, Arkansas. My creative journey began at 13 when I bought my first 3D printer, turning it into a lifeline of innovation during the COVID lockdowns.

What started as playground experiments in terrain modeling quickly grew into Elevon’s signature 3D trail maps. By my senior year of high school, I was professionally crafting custom trophies for top teams at the Arkansas NICA State Championship—my very first real-world customers. That early taste of entrepreneurship and every piece of feedback that followed inspired me to bring Elevon to campus and beyond.
