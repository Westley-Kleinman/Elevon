About Elevon

Turning topographical trails into custom 3D-printed models

Hello! I'm Westley Kleinman, a mechanical engineering student at Duke University with a passion for mapping and design. I founded Elevon to bring trail systems to life through detailed, custom 3D prints that capture every contour and peak.

When I'm not in the CAD lab, you'll find me mountain biking, exploring new trails, or collaborating with fellow cycling enthusiasts. Let's elevate your adventure with Elevon.
