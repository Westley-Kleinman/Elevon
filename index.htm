About Elevon

Elevon was founded by outdoor enthusiasts on a mission to make trail exploration tangible. With a background in mechanical engineering and a passion for mapping, our founder transforms flat trail data into stunning 3D sculptures you can hold in your hands.

Whether you’re commemorating a race victory, showcasing your favorite hike, or crafting a unique gift, Elevon delivers precision-made, display-ready models that bring your adventures to life.
